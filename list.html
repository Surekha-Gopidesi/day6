Task7:omlette Recipe List

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • salt
  • 2 onions
  • green chilli
  • turmeric
  • oil

process of making

  1. Beat the 2 Eggs and added into a bowl.
  2. Add salt for taste and littele turmeric powder in that.
  3. Beat well.
  4. Then Add chopped onions and green chilli in that and stir well so all ingredients blend evenly.
  5. place non stick pan on medium flame, add one tablespoon of oil.
  6. After heating the pan pour the egg mixture into the pan and spread evenly.
  7. Cook on medium flame for 1or 2 mins and flip the omlette and do the same process other side.
  8. slide the omlette in to the plate and Enjoy.
